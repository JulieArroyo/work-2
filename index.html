Dave's Guide Service

Designing A Fishing Charter

Dave's Guide Service is a one man operation out of Branson, MO. He wanted an updated website to compete with the new fishing charters near him. He has been in business for years, and really knows his fishing. With this design I wanted to convey a fun atmosphere with a down-to-earth captain.

The Idea

With this design I wanted to convey a fun atmosphere with a down-to-earth captain. So I knew earth tones were the way to go here.

The Design

  • Background Photo - The beautiful Missouri River. What better way to show the user where they will be fishing. I used Photoshop to blend that background into wood planks right at the water line in the photo.
  • Slider Images - The slides here show not only happy anglers, but also the great fish to catch.
  • Services - Three large photos directing users to services & pricing pages. Having thses here helps the user decide quickly that they want to book a charter.
  • About - A small paragraph that quickly explains the business with a picture of the boat that Dave uses. Now the user knows a little more of what to expect on a charter.
  • Pricing - A quick row of pricing for the most common charters. Adding a button here makes it easy for a user to contact Dave for more details.
    To break up the wood planks, and also to draw attention to the prices, I added another Missouri River photo as the background.
  • What To Bring - One of the most useful sections for both the custer and the client. I have a weather widget sfor the upcoming week, and a nice-to-have list of ways to prepare for the trips.
